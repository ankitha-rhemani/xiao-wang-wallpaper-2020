# xiao-wang-wallpaper-2020
A fan-made wallpaper application featuring high-quality wallpapers of Xiao Zhan and Wang Yibo. This was my first published Android app, launched in 2020 and maintained until 2024, reaching 66,000+ installs on the Google Play Store, and marked the beginning of my app development journey.

## Features
- View high-quality wallpapers
- Download wallpapers to device
- Set wallpapers directly from the app
- Push notifications for new content updates
- Lightweight and simple UI

## Tech Stack
- Kodular – No-code Android app development
- OneSignal – Push notifications
- Online image hosting – Wallpaper fetching

## Impact
- 66,000+ total installs on Google Play Store
- Active from 2020 to 2024
- Consistent positive user feedback over multiple years
